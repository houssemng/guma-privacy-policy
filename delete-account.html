Delete Your Rydo Account

You can request the deletion of your Rydo account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

Option 1 — From the app:

  1. Open the Rydo app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Option 2 — By email:

  1. Send an email to support@rydo.tn
  2. Use subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address linked to your account
  4. We will process your request within 7 days

What data gets deleted

  • Your name, email, and profile photo
  • Your trip history
  • Your chat messages
  • Your location data

Data retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal and security purposes, after which it is permanently deleted from our servers.
