Privacy Policy — Ryde Carpool App Last updated: May 2025 Ryde ("we", "our", or "us") is a carpooling application connecting drivers and passengers in Tunisia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. 1. Information We Collect Phone Number: Used for account registration and login via OTP verification. Full Name: Used to identify you on your profile. Profile Photo: Optional photo uploaded by the user for profile identification. National ID (CIN) Photo: Collected for driver identity verification purposes only. Location Data: Real-time GPS location is used to match drivers and passengers, display routes, and calculate trip details. Device Information: Basic device data collected for app functionality and push notifications. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Create and manage your account Match drivers with passengers based on routes Send trip notifications and updates Verify driver identity and ensure platform safety Improve app performance and user experience 3. Data Sharing We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data with trusted service providers: Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and push notifications Google Maps: Location services and route calculations 4. Location Data We collect location data while the app is in use to provide carpooling services such as matching and navigation. Location is never collected in the background without your knowledge. You can disable location access anytime from your device settings. 5. Data Retention We retain your data as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. 6. Children's Privacy Ryde is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. 7. Security We use industry-standard security measures such as: Firebase security rules Encrypted data transmission Access control policies to protect your personal information. 8. Your Rights You have the right to: Access your personal data Correct inaccurate information Request deletion of your data To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below. 9.
