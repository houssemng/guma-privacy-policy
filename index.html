Privacy Policy for Guma

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

Guma ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Guma carpooling mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Important Disclosure: Guma collects Location data and Identity documents to facilitate safe carpooling coordination in Tunisia. This data is handled with the highest security standards.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Types of Data Collected

  • Personal Data: While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information including your Name and Phone Number.
  • Identity Verification (Drivers): To ensure the safety of our community, we require drivers to upload photos of their National Identity Card (CIN) and Car Insurance. These photos are used solely for verification.
  • Location Data: We use your location to match you with nearby rides and allow passengers to track their drivers. We may use background location if you are an active driver.

2. Use of Data

Guma uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To notify you about changes to our Service.
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features (Chat, Bookings).
  • To provide user support.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service and detect security issues.

3. Transfer of Data

Your information, including Personal Data, is processed using Google Firebase servers. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of information to these secure servers.

4. Disclosure of Data

Your name and phone number will be visible to the other party (Driver or Passenger) only once a ride is coordinated or booked, to allow for communication via phone or WhatsApp.

5. Service Providers

We employ third-party companies to facilitate our Service:

  • Google Maps: Used for mapping and location services.
  • Firebase: Used for authentication, database storage, and push notifications.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can delete your chat history and account directly within the app settings. For any other data requests, please contact us.
