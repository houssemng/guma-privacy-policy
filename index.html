Privacy Policy — Guma Carpool App

Last updated: May 2025

Guma ("we", "our", or "us") is a carpooling application connecting drivers and passengers in Tunisia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Used for account registration and login via OTP verification.
  • Full Name: Used to identify you on your profile.
  • Profile Photo: Optional photo uploaded by the user for profile identification.
  • National ID (CIN) Photo: Collected for driver identity verification purposes only.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location is used to match drivers and passengers, display routes, and calculate trip details.
  • Device Information: Basic device data collected for app functionality and push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match drivers with passengers based on routes
  • To send trip notifications and updates
  • To verify driver identity and ensure platform safety
  • To improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and push notifications
  • Google Maps: For location and routing services

4. Location Data

We collect location data while the app is in use to provide carpooling services. Location is never collected in the background without your knowledge. You can disable location access at any time from your device settings.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Guma is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase security rules and encrypted data transmission to protect your information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us to exercise these rights.
